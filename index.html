How It Works

Our technology uses state-of-the-art AI to enable seamless communication across languages.

1

Speech Recognition

Whisper ASR technology converts spoken words to text with high accuracy, even in noisy environments.

2

Translation

NLLB-200 model instantly translates text to the target language with contextual understanding.

3

Speech Synthesis

MMS-TTS technology converts translated text to natural-sounding speech in the target language.
